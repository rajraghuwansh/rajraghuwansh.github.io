Recently, I had the opportunity to attend **AWS Summit Paris** on behalf of my team at **Stellantis France**.

It was my first time participating in an event of this scale, and it was an incredible experience to be immersed in the **tech ecosystem of France and Europe**.

<!--more-->

The summit was a **full-day event** with more than **50 companies showcasing their technologies and solutions** through talks, presentations, and booths.

I had the chance to meet and listen to professionals working across many domains including:

- Cloud infrastructure
- Encryption and cybersecurity
- Automotive technologies
- SaaS and PaaS platforms
- Supply chain systems
- Artificial intelligence and machine learning

A major theme of this year’s summit was **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and the **Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)** approach used to address some of their practical challenges.

It was fascinating to see how organizations are integrating **AI-driven solutions with AWS cloud infrastructure** to build scalable and intelligent systems.

The event provided a great opportunity to learn about new developments in **cloud computing, AI, and industry applications**, while also connecting with professionals from across the European tech community.

## Event Highlights

- 50+ companies presenting cutting-edge technologies
- Focus on **LLMs and RAG architectures**
- Discussions on **cloud-native AI systems**
- Insights into **automotive and industrial AI applications**
- Networking with professionals from across Europe
